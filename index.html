Projects

ThreadsClone App

ThreadsClone is a social media app developed using SwiftUI Framework replication original Threads App by Meta. It allows users to share text updates, photos, and videos with their close friends. The app emphasizes private, close-knit communication, and includes various features.

Uber App

Uber App is a iOS application that streamlines the ridesharing process by helping passengers connecting them with the drivers. The app also offers features like ride scheduling, fare estimation, and the option to choose different vehicle types.
